About Us

Few years back our team set to find out solutions to the problem of plastic pollution in Kerala. It took more than 2 years of research and finally Nexge was lauched to provide replacements to single use plastics.

Today, Nexge is a widely accepted brand across the Indian subcontinent and also sold outside India in UAE, UK, Africa, Qatar and Sri Lanka. 'For us, the joy of converting even one customer has carried the same value as has that of having many thousand satisfied customers today.'

Our approach to packaging has always been centered around good design and in our efforts to make sustainable packaging more user friendly we are undergoing massive research & development continuously at our head office to provide the best possible innovations in the market.

Apart from being sustainable, at Nexge, we also give great importance to quality & safety in packaging. As all our packaging is designed to come in food contact we ensure products we sell are safe tested as well as tested for heavy metals & other contaminants. We follow a strict guideline & take responsibility for everything that leaves our warehouse & reaches our customers.
